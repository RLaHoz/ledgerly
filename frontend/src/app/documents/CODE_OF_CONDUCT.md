# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give clear, actionable technical feedback.
- Focus on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting comments, or bad-faith reviews
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict access for violations.

## Reporting
To report conduct issues, open a private security/discussion channel with maintainers (see `SECURITY.md` process).
